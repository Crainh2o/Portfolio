I'm notary public in the state of Georgia. I can notarize in all counties of the state of Georgia. A notary public of the common law is a public officer constituted by law to serve the public in non-contentious matters usually concerned with general financial transactions, estates, deeds, powers-of-attorney, and foreign and international business.

What to Bring to a Notary

The completed document you wish to notarize.

Valid photo ID that meets state requirements.

Any other individuals who will be signing the document. They must bring proper ID as well.

Payment for notarial services.

Acceptable Identification

A driver's license or non-drivers identification card issued by the state of Georgia, another state, a territory of the United States, Canada, or Mexico.

A United States Passport

A forgien passport stamped by the United States Bureau of Citizenship and Immigration Services.
